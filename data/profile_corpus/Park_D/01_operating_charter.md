# Park D — Operating Charter (excerpt, revised 2024-Q3)

**Park designation.** Park D is the regional reference park, operated at
the template scale used for cluster planning and benchmarking. PV, ESS,
EV, and flexible-load capacities are at the unit-scale reference and the
park serves as the comparison anchor for the other cluster members.

**Operator authority.** The operator has standard authority to dispatch
internal assets and to participate in inter-park trade within the
published regional rules. There is no park-specific override or special
mandate.

**Demand-side coordination.** HVAC and shiftable service loads follow the
regional comfort default. EV charging follows the regional best-effort
fulfilment policy with a guaranteed daily energy quota.

**Inter-park interaction.** Park D participates in the inter-park trade
mechanism on a price-driven basis. The operator submits offers and bids
when on-site flexibility headroom indicates a feasible position; the
park does not actively pursue a directional role.

**Trade orientation.** Trades are accepted when the projected economic
margin exceeds the standard regional transaction fee. Carbon performance
is monitored but is not the primary scheduling objective.

**Reporting cadence.** Park D submits the standard regional reporting
package and does not publish any additional voluntary disclosures.
