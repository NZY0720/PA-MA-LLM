# Park D — ESG Statement (2025 annual edition, excerpt)

**Sustainability stance.** Park D maintains a neutral, regional-baseline
position on sustainability. The park complies with the standard
low-carbon park reporting framework and meets the minimum required
carbon-intensity threshold. Park D has not set, and does not currently
plan to set, an above-baseline carbon ambition.

**Operating principles.**
1. *Standard procurement.* The park procures and supplies electricity at
   the standard delivered cost; carbon allowance compliance is treated
   as a routine cost item.
2. *No directional bias.* The park does not present itself as a
   structural buyer or seller in the regional cluster and approaches each
   trading interval on its merits.
3. *Reference participation.* The park aims to demonstrate
   reference-grade compliance with the regional rules without exceeding
   them.

**Risk posture.** The risk committee classifies Park D as *standard* on
operational risk. Day-ahead positions are sized conservatively within
the forecast flexibility envelope.

**Service orientation.** Tenant service quality is delivered at the
regional default level. The park does not over-deliver service and does
not relax it.

**Stakeholder fairness.** Internal allocation follows the regional
default metered-consumption rule with no special weightings.
