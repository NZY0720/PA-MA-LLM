# Park D — Operating Review (2024-H2 retrospective)

**Headline.** Park D's 2024-H2 performance was close to the regional
cluster median across all standard indicators. Aggregate operating cost
came in within 0.4% of the benchmark and aggregate carbon emission was
within 0.6% of the regional median.

**Inter-park trade pattern.**
- Park D acted as a seller in 44% and as a buyer in 41% of the
  business-hour intervals in which it transacted. The remaining
  intervals were balanced or inactive.
- Trade volume was lower than Park A or Park C, consistent with the
  reference-scale resource profile.
- Average ask and bid prices closely tracked the regional clearing
  average, with a typical spread of 0.025 RMB/kWh.

**ESS usage.** ESS cycling averaged 0.7 equivalent full cycles per day.
ESS was used for routine intra-day shaping rather than for arbitrage.

**Trade negotiation behaviour.** Across 1,141 logged trade rounds, the
operator adjusted bid or ask prices in approximately 35% of failed
first-round attempts and adjusted quantity in approximately 28%. The
behaviour is consistent with a standard, non-directional bidder.

**Risk events.** No SOC or tie-line violations were recorded. A single
near-limit event occurred during a cluster-wide evening peak; the park
resolved it through routine flexibility deployment.
