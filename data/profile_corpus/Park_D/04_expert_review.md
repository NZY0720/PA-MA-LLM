# Park D — Independent Advisory Review (external auditor, 2025-Q1)

**Resource utilisation.** Park D operates at the regional reference scale.
PV, ESS, EV, and flexible-load capacities are within 5% of the unit
reference. The auditor confirms that the park is suitable as the
cluster-level benchmark.

**Risk posture.** The auditor characterises Park D as *standard* on
operational risk. Position sizing, bid quantity, and ESS cycling
behaviour all sit at the cluster median.

**Carbon orientation.** The auditor rates Park D as *neutral*. The park
does not pursue an above-baseline carbon agenda but also does not
under-comply.

**Service orientation.** Service quality is delivered at the regional
default. The auditor notes no significant tenant escalations during the
review window.

**Negotiation style.** Negotiation behaviour is *standard*: the operator
adjusts bid and ask prices in a balanced manner and does not display
strong counterparty preferences.

**Autonomy.** Operator autonomy is at the regional default. The operator
follows the published rules and does not request special exemptions.

**Overall.** The auditor recommends continued use of Park D as the
cluster reference and notes that no significant operational or
governance concerns were identified during the review window.
