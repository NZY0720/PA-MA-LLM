# Park D — Regulatory and Policy Alignment Memo

**Provincial low-carbon park scheme.** Park D is registered as a Tier-2
participant under the provincial low-carbon park certification. Tier-2
status sets compliance at the regional baseline and serves as the
reference category for the regional cluster.

**Carbon allowance posture.** Park D is allocated the baseline allowance
factor (1.00× the regional benchmark intensity). The park's expected
allowance position is near-balanced; small annual surplus or deficit is
settled through the standard regional credit market.

**Inter-park trade obligations.** Park D follows the standard inter-park
trade rules without any special obligations. The park is required to
submit an offer or bid only when on-site flexibility headroom is
non-trivial.

**Tariff and incentive alignment.** Park D does not receive any
park-specific tariff or incentive. The park is subject to the standard
regional electricity tariff and the standard carbon allowance settlement.

**Reporting and verification.** Standard regional reporting cadence and
standard verification apply.

**Operator autonomy and constraints.** Operator autonomy is at the
regional default. Strategic decisions are subject to the standard
governance process.
